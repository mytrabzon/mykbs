# MYKBS - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: 26 February 2026**

The MyKBS application is owned and operated by **LITXTECH LLC**, **TOPRAK TRAVEL TOURISM**, and **LITXTECH LTD** (collectively referred to as the "Company").

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected, processed, stored, and protected within the MyKBS mobile application.

MyKBS is designed for identity verification and security purposes. The application may process MRZ (Machine Readable Zone) data from passports or identity documents. **MyKBS does not store or upload photographs of identity or passport documents.** Only text data derived from the MRZ lines (document number, date of birth, expiry date, country code, etc.) is processed.

---

## 1. Information We Process

The application may process:

- Data derived from MRZ on passports or ID cards (raw MRZ images or document photos are not stored)
- Passport or identity document number
- Date of birth
- Document expiry date
- Issuing country code
- Verification result and transaction status
- Device information (device model, OS version, app version)
- Technical log data (sensitive fields are masked)

**Identity data is processed only with explicit user consent.**

---

## 2. Purpose of Processing

Data is processed solely for:

- Identity verification
- Account security
- Fraud and forgery prevention
- Security analysis
- Legal and regulatory compliance

MyKBS does not use identity data for advertising, marketing, or profiling. **Identity data is never sold to third parties.**

---

## 3. Data Retention

- Verification data may be retained for a **limited period** for security and legal compliance.
- After the retention period, data is **deleted** or **anonymized**.
- Technical logs may be kept for a defined period for security purposes.

---

## 4. Data Security

MyKBS applies the following measures:

- All data transmission is protected with **TLS/SSL encryption**.
- Sensitive data is stored on **secure server-side infrastructure**.
- Identity data is **not permanently stored** on the user's device.
- **Access control** mechanisms are used to prevent unauthorized access.
- Sensitive fields are **masked** in logs and reports.

---

## 5. Third-Party Service Providers

Data may be shared with technical infrastructure providers (e.g. hosting, databases) only to the extent necessary to provide the service. Such providers are bound by data protection obligations.

---

## 6. Your Rights

You may:

- **Request access** to your personal data
- **Request deletion** of your data
- **Object** to certain processing

Contact: **support@litxtech.com**

---

## 7. Disclaimer

MyKBS is **not an official government verification service.** Verification results provided by the application are for **internal security** and identity verification purposes only and do not constitute legal or official certification.
